Our Story

Born from a belief
in transparent elections

Voter-Pulse started over a year ago with a simple frustration — conducting an election survey was complicated, expensive, and inaccessible to most people. Existing tools were either too technical or too costly for independent organisers and media houses.

We set out to change that. Our team built a platform that lets anyone — from a local community group to a national TV channel — create a professional election survey in minutes, publish it to the world, and watch the results come in live.

Today, thousands of users recommend Voter-Pulse as the easiest way to gauge the public's pulse before an election. And we're just getting started.
